Site contents:

Site contents make all information accessible by opening a popup window that includes links on all different diagrams and objects

  • Click on Site Contents to gain access to hyperlinks including site content organized according to category.
  • Click on the (+) sign on the left of each category to expand the table of content and to view all elements of the diagram.

In the list of objects, click on the object you want.

Site content provides access to information about the report's objects. When you press the site content button, a window will pop up containing the reports objects which are grouped according category.

A hyperlinked table of content provides access to information about the selected catgory.

  • To expand TOC, press the (+) sign on the left of the hyperlink.
  • To collapse TOC, press the (-) sign on the left of the hyperlink.

Once the window is open, you can view the different pages, using the hyperlinked TOC.
